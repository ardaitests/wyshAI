Treehouse Home Estimator

Launch the chatbot in the lower right corner to find out what Treehouse estimates your house is worth.

This demo features:

  • Custom UI design
  • Custom AI agent scripting
  • OpenAI / ChatGPT integration
  • Data collected and saved to Google Drive and Supabase (hosted database)
  • Zillow property detail lookup via API, including home price and square footage
  • Photo upload to Google Drive
